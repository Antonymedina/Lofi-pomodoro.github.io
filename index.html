Lofi Pomodoro

Lofi Pomodoro is a web application that combines the Pomodoro technique with relaxing lofi music to help you stay focused and productive. It features a customizable timer, an aesthetically pleasing interface, and various lofi music tracks to choose from, making it the perfect companion for students, freelancers, and remote workers.
